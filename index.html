SnapContact

Face to Contacts • Clean contact photos in seconds

SnapContact makes it fast and effortless to add a clean face photo to your contacts.

Choose a photo, let SnapContact detect faces directly on your device, select the right person, refine the crop if needed, and save it to a new or existing contact — all in just a few taps.

Ideal for events, meetups, or any situation where group photos make saving contacts harder than it should be.

Built with privacy and speed in mind: • On-device face detection — no uploads, no servers • Works with both single and group photos • Auto-crop with manual crop and zoom controls • Save photos to new or existing contacts • Minimal, premium interface designed for fast workflows

Your photos never leave your device. All processing happens locally, and contacts are only saved when you choose.
