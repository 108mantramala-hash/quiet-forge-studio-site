SnapContact

SnapContact makes it fast and effortless to add a clean face photo to your contacts. Choose a photo, let SnapContact detect faces directly on your device, select the right person, refine the crop if needed, and save it to a new or existing contact — all in just a few taps.

About SnapContact

What it does

SnapContact helps you quickly identify the correct face from a photo and turn it into a polished contact photo. Perfect for events, meetups, and group photos.

  • Works with single and group photos
  • Auto-crop with manual crop and zoom controls
  • Save to new or existing contacts

Privacy-first

Built with privacy and speed in mind.

  • On-device face detection — no uploads, no servers
  • Your photos never leave your device
  • Contacts are saved only when you choose
Status: Live iOS + Android

Designed for fast workflows

A minimal, premium interface designed for quick results — open, pick, crop, save.

  • Clean UI, fewer steps
  • Great for frequent contact updates
  • Built for reliability
