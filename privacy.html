Privacy Policy SnapContact

Quiet Forge Studio • Last updated: January 15, 2026

Quiet Forge Studio (“we”, “our”, or “us”) built the SnapContact app as a utility application. This Privacy Policy explains how SnapContact handles information when you use the app.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how data is handled.

Information We Collect

Photos & Face Detection

SnapContact requests access to your device’s photo library only when you choose to select a photo.

  • Face detection is performed entirely on your device
  • Photos are never uploaded, transmitted, or stored on any server
  • SnapContact performs face detection only, not face recognition
  • The app does not identify individuals or infer identity

Photos are processed locally only to help you manually crop and save a contact photo.

Face Data Handling

SnapContact does not collect, store, retain, or transmit face data.

  • No biometric templates are created
  • No facial measurements or identifiers are stored
  • No face data is saved after photo processing

Face detection results exist only temporarily in memory to assist with photo cropping and are discarded immediately after use.

Contacts

SnapContact accesses your contacts only when you explicitly choose to save a photo to a new or existing contact.

  • Contacts are stored only on your device
  • No contact data is uploaded to any server
  • No contact data is shared with third parties

Information We Do NOT Collect

SnapContact does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Contact data to our servers
  • Contact photos to our servers
  • Face data or face detection results
  • Facial features, biometric data, or biometric templates
  • Face identifiers
  • Personal identity information
  • Photo metadata (e.g., EXIF) for tracking purposes
  • Location data

How We Use Information

Information is used only to:

  • Enable face detection and photo cropping locally on your device
  • Save a photo to a contact only at your request

Data Sharing

SnapContact does not share photos, face data, or contact data with third parties. The app does not upload photos or contacts to any server.

Data Retention

  • Photos: Used only while you are selecting/cropping; not retained by the app after the action.
  • Face detection results: Processed ephemerally in memory and discarded immediately after use.
  • Contacts: Saved to your device’s system Contacts only when you confirm saving.

Data Storage & Security

  • All photo processing happens on your device
  • No images, face data, or contacts are stored on our servers
  • SnapContact does not require accounts or sign-in

User Control

You are always in control:

  • You choose which photo to use
  • You choose which person/face to select and crop
  • You choose whether to save a contact photo
  • You may revoke photo or contacts permissions at any time via your device settings
  • You can delete or edit contacts using your device’s Contacts app

Children’s Privacy

SnapContact is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
