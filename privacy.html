Privacy Policy SnapContact

Quiet Forge Studio • Last updated: [January 06, 2026]

Quiet Forge Studio (“we”, “our”, or “us”) built the SnapContact app as a utility application. This Privacy Policy explains how SnapContact handles information when you use the app.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how data is handled.

Information We Collect

Photos & Face Detection

SnapContact requests access to your device’s photo library only when you choose to select a photo.

  • Face detection is performed entirely on your device
  • Photos are never uploaded, transmitted, or stored on any server
  • SnapContact does not perform face recognition
  • The app does not identify individuals, infer identity, or store facial data

All photo processing happens locally and only to help you manually crop and save contact photos.

Contacts

SnapContact saves contact photos only when you explicitly choose to save them. The app does not read, modify, or transmit your contacts without your direct action.

Analytics & Crash Data (Optional)

To improve app stability and performance, we may collect limited, non-personal usage data, such as:

  • App crashes
  • Basic event counts (e.g., feature usage)

This data:

  • Does not include photos or contact details
  • Is not linked to personal identity
  • Is used solely to improve app quality

Analytics are optional and minimal.

Information We Do NOT Collect

SnapContact does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Contact photos
  • Facial features or biometric data
  • Face identifiers
  • Personal identity information
  • Photo metadata
  • Location data

How We Use Information

Information is used only to:

  • Enable face detection and photo cropping locally
  • Save photos to contacts at your request
  • Improve app stability and performance (if analytics are enabled)

Data Storage & Security

  • All photo processing happens on your device
  • No images or face data are stored on our servers
  • Any optional analytics data is handled securely by trusted service providers

User Control

You are always in control:

  • You choose which photo to use
  • You choose which face to crop
  • You choose whether to save a contact photo
  • You may revoke photo permissions at any time via your device settings

Children’s Privacy

SnapContact is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
